    AI detects 6 major skin data

    Six major skin problems through AI image recognition Machine learning (300W+ clinical data) makes the detection more and more accurate At present, from 360 skin solutions, we accurately recommend the corresponding solutions for users
    • 1-Skin color
    • 2-Stains
    • 3-Pores
    • 4-wrinkle
    • 5-sensitive
    • 6-PBottom pigment

      Skin detection Show the original photo, determine the skin color, and judge the sunburn resistance

      Spot detection Intelligently label the distribution position of color spots on the face, and judge the severity of the proportion of color spots in the detection area

      Pore detection Intelligently mark the location of large pores on the face, and judge the number and severity of large pores

      Wrinkle detection Smart mark the location of wrinkles on the face to determine the number and severity of wrinkles

      Sensitive detection Healthy skin is pink and white. Due to inflammation, acne, squeezing, and other reasons, the bottom of the skin will show local dot-like red symptoms. By observing the sensitivity map, it can be judged whether there is inflammation in the skin, and those areas are dehydrated and dry.

      Underlayer pigment detection You can see the accumulation of skin pigments from the epidermis to the dermis, which is helpful for us to read the types of spots and potential pigments
