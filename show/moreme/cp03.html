      Irradiate soft scattered light to the face from all directions to evenly illuminate all the details of the face without shadows or highlighting the special problems of different layers of the skin. Provide an objective and intuitive image basis for comprehensive evaluation of facial skin.

      We are usually unable to clearly observe the underside of the skin epidermis due to the strong direct reflection of the skin surface. In the polarization mode, a special cross-polarizing lens group is used to effectively reduce the direct reflected light, allowing us to observe the uneven skin tone under the epidermis and the microvascular structure hidden under the shiny skin. In polarization mode, aging skin may be more transparent, revealing irregular skin features under the skin epidermis.

      Skin texture pattern enhances the visibility of skin surface texture. This mode is the opposite of the polarization map. In this mode, the parallel polarizing lens group reduces the visibility of the skin under the skin surface layer, strengthens the surface optical reflection, and shows the surface texture details, pore structure and the first appearance of fineness. Pattern.

      In the sun damage mode, a small amount of harmless ultraviolet rays can be irradiated to the facial skin. These invisible rays can penetrate the skin surface. Below the skin surface, cells and tissues have the natural function of converting these invisible light into visible fluorescence, thereby effectively turning the skin into a luminous body. Because the light is produced in the skin instead of the usual reflected light, the skin problems hidden under the skin surface can be clearly observed.

      The simulated Wood's light picture is named after Robert William Wood, the inventor of the original Fifth Lamp. With the help of 320-400nm ultraviolet light, skin problems with abnormal pigmentation can be found, such as vitiligo, identification of various spots: various fungal and bacterial infections Diagnosis of sexual skin diseases, such as various skin areas; it also has a good role in judging the secretion area of acne and skin oil.

      Due to physiological aging and light damage, the skin will have uneven skin tone. Unlike the innate uniform skin tone, local pigmentation will occur in each area of the face and the skin tone will also change. The skin tone mode focuses on the subtle differences in skin tone and pigmentation, so that uneven skin tone affects age better than wrinkles, sagging skin and other signs of surface aging.

      Healthy skin is pink and white. Due to inflammation, acne, squeezing and other reasons, the bottom layer of the skin appears spotted and red, which needs to be improved by treatment; large areas of the face caused by sunlight, allergies, blood circulation after exercise, etc. Red, as time and circulation slow down, it will disappear automatically; the reactions in the two situations are completely different.

      The brown picture, you can clearly see the distribution of melanin, please check the melanin analysis to find out the type of spots (chloasma, tan, age spots), whether there are hidden pigments in the bottom of the skin, whether there has been any treatment for the spots, and what is it like Treatments (product peels, light treatments, etc.).

      When looking at skin problems in different modes, our human eyes cannot match the coordinate points of each mode one by one, and sometimes misjudgment may be made because of coordinate errors. The coordinate map gives approximate coordinates for each point on the photo, thereby reducing misjudgments.

      According to the basic state of the skin, combined with the face analysis data of the first 9 modes, the DJM cloud computer can accurately calculate the distribution and state of the future spots on the face, and understand the development trend of the spots earlier.
